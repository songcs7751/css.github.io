Changseob Song

I'm a PhD candidate at Carnegie Mellon University, where I work with Prof. Inseung Kang at MetaMobility Lab. I am working on exoskeleton personalization using machine learning, computer vision, and physics-informed musculoskeletal simulation. I did my BS and MS at Korea University, where I was advised by Prof. Shinsuk Park. During my MS, I worked at Korea Institute of Science and Technology, where I was advised by Dr. Donghyun Hwang and worked on magnetic field-driven variable stiffness mechanisms.
